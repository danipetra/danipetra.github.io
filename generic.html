About Me

"If you're going to try, go all the way. Otherwise, don't even start."
(Charles Bukowski, Factotum)

I was born on June 25, 1996. Since I was a child I have pursued my two passions: Programming and Making and Playing music, which allowed me to give space to my creativity and my wits. I've always loved video games and music for their capacity to entertain and excite people, while allowing who creates them to express his creativity. So during my studies, I grew the passion for Game Design and Development and started making games, mainly with Unity (but also with the Unreal Engine). Since then I never stopped. In 2022 I graduated in Computer Science at University of Studies of Molise, by defending a thesis in the subject of Game Design and Development, named "Detection and Visualization with Graphs of network traffic anomalies" developed in collaboration with the company Dromic. This experience allowed me to combine my passion for making games and for new tecnologies like VR, with my experience as data analyst. In 2020 and 2021 I held the role of Data analyst, which taught me to meet deadlines and manage large responsibilities.

In my free time I enjoy singing, playing the piano and guitar, reading books and practicing various sports, including skiing and swimming. In the past I was the frontman of a band, this experience allowed me to improve my creativity and empathy.

Professional Skills

Unity and C#

Deep knowledge of the Unity engine and C# programming language. Knowledge of physic, lighting, collision, and VR components. Experience in VR application development and in the usage of the XR Interaction Toolkit. Experience in mobile development, advertising (with UnityAds) and the release process. Experience in multiplayer development with the networking library.

Python

Advanced knowledge of Python usage and data analysis libraryes as pandas and numphy. Experience in developing Restful APIs with the Flask micro-framework.

Javascript, HTML, CSS

Knowledge of web development, with the most of my experience with front-end development. Knowledge of the Bootstrap, Ionic frameworks. Experience with the D3 data library and the G6 Visualization engine.

Unreal Engine and C++

Good knowledge of the Unreal Engine, its functionalities and with the C++ programming language. Experience with the blueprint development.

.NET and C#
